RASM
Robotic Adaptive Screen Mount

About Us

The RASM's primary function is to provide automated 6 degree-of-freedom pose control of a Tobii Screen. The Tobii screen functions as a computer with built-in eye-tracking. This allows patients with tetraplegia and quadriplegia to easily operate both standard applications, like web browsing, hospital-specific applications and as simple as calling for a nurse. The RASM will provide a robotic replacement to the currently used passive mechanical arm for holding the Tobii screen in front of a patient's face, independent of whether the patient is in a hospital bed or a wheelchair. This will aid in patients having more successful and faster recoveries and will lessen the daily operating costs of hospitals.
